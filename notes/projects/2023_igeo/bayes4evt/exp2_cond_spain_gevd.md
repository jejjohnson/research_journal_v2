In this experiment, we look at the conditional prediction of each station using the GEVD distribution

$$
\begin{aligned}
\mu(t;\theta) &= \mu_0 + \mu_1 t \\
\sigma(t;\theta) &= \sigma_0 \\
\kappa(t;\theta) &= \kappa_0
\end{aligned}
$$
